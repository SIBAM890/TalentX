Data Scientist – AI Platform (3+ Years Experience)

Company: Nexora AI Labs
Location: Bengaluru / Remote (India)
Type: Full-Time | Permanent

About Nexora AI Labs:
Nexora AI Labs is building the next generation of AI-powered SaaS products for the BFSI and EdTech sectors.

---

Job Summary:
We are looking for a skilled Data Scientist with 3+ years of hands-on experience in machine learning,
deep learning, and NLP. You will work directly with the AI Platform team to design, build, and deploy
ML models that power our recommendation engine, risk engine, and personalization systems.

---

Key Responsibilities:
- Design and implement end-to-end ML pipelines from data ingestion to deployment
- Build NLP models for text classification, named entity recognition, and sentiment analysis
- Develop recommendation systems using collaborative filtering and content-based methods
- Optimize model performance using techniques like hyperparameter tuning and feature engineering
- Deploy models using Docker, FastAPI, and cloud platforms (AWS SageMaker / GCP Vertex AI)
- Monitor model drift, retrain pipelines, and ensure production reliability
- Collaborate with cross-functional teams (product, engineering, design)
- Communicate insights clearly to non-technical stakeholders
